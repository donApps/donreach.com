Celebrate the launch of our new social share count API with a 50% discount!

By Adam Bouqdib 22 May 2017

Welcome to the all new donreach.com. It’s been a while since anything noteworthy happened here so I’m all the more pleased to break the silence with not only a flashy new website but also the news most of you have been waiting for: our premium social share count API has finally launched!

Premium Social API Launched

Due to ever rising popularity, our open social share count API has been plagued by bad performance for quite some time now and many of you have asked us for a long-term solution, so I’m happy to announce our most-requested feature by far: a lightning fast premium social share count API service with more social networking services supported than ever!

Here are some of the highlights:

Get 50% off for 6 months

To celebrate the launch of the new API we’re giving anyone who signs up by the 30th of June a whopping 50% off their first 6 months subscription.

To get the discount simply enter the coupon code LAUNCH50 during checkout.

Improved performance

To cope with the millions of people accessing our API every month we’ve rewritten it yet again, benchmarking our code at every step of the way to squeeze out every last bit of performance possible. On top of that, we’re applying a very generous ratio of premium API users per server to ensure a reliable service, even during peak-times.

18 new social networking services supported

That’s right, the amount of different social share counts our API can fetch has nearly doubled! We’ve expanded our portfolio of supported providers to include major non-english social networks from around the world including China, Russia, Spain, Poland & Bulgaria, just to name a few.

Follower counts (BETA)

Starting now you can also track social profiles’ follower counts through our API. This functionality is still in BETA though and likely to change.
